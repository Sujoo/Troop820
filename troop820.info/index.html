Welcome to the public homepage of Troop 820. Troop 820 is sponsored by St. Thomas More Catholic Church. We meet Thursday evenings from 7:30-9:00 p.m. at St. Thomas More School, 920 Carmichael Street in Chapel Hill. The first Thursday of each month is for Troop Leadership only. The last Thursday includes Board of Reviews.

Only very general information is contained on this page. Detailed information may only be found on the Yahoo Groups Troop 820 page, which is password protected. If you are a member of the troop or a parent, please use the link below to send an e-mail and request an invitation to the Yahoo Group which contains a Troop Calendar, rosters, photos, files, etc. You should also request that you be added to the Troop e-mail list. Thanks.

Boy Scout Information

Troop 820 has approximately 40 scouts, ages 11-17, and several Assistant Scoutmasters. Our patrols are the Barracudas, Cobras, Flaming Eagles, Panthers, Scorpions, and Leadership. We have an active monthly camping program.

183 Scouts have become Eagle Scouts through Troop 820. Please click on the link below for a complete list as well as some helpful information for those Scouts pursuing Eagle.

Troop 820 has been served by twelve Scoutmasters since being founded on March 19, 1969. They are: Harry Jensen, Bill Chapell, Jim Wells, John Humber, John Overton, Alex Bell, Doug Leigh, Tim Taft, Bob Verne, Thor Fjellstedt, Alfred Reid, and Ed Bedford.
